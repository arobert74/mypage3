The Meadowlands complex consists of three major facilities - the Meadowlands Racetrack, the Meadowlands Arena, and Giants Stadium. The stadium is known solely as Giants Stadium and the Meadowlands Arena is the enclosed building where the NJ Nets and NJ Devils play. They all share the same parking facilities.

 
Here is the past History of the New York Giants Stadium Locations

Location Years
Polo Grounds, New York 1925 - 1955
Yankee Stadium, New York 1956 - September 1973
Yale Bowl, Connecticut October 1973 - 1974
Shea Stadium, New York 1975
Giants Stadium, New Jersey 1976 - 2009
MetLife Stadium, New Jersey 2010 - present
